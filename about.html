About Us

Aastha Arora is a Choreographer, A dancer who loves to teach. Aastha has been trained in Trained in Bollywood,Jazz, Contemporary, Salsa and various other dance forms. A Teacher by heart, a fitness freak, Gym enthusiastic and a yoga practitioner by heart. Dance Teacher with GD Goenka for 2 years. Conceptualised & Choreographed over 70 Weddings over 3 years.

Choreography was not my first career. Aastha has pursued her Masters in Brand Management and PR. She has worked with Various prestigious firms as aBrand Manager and even opened her own Jewelery firm. Being a Dance & Functional Fitness Instructor ( Zumba ) and a Dance teacher for 5 years she finally took the leap of faith and went full time into her passion. She has been a part of various Dance festivals and taught over 5000 + students .
