About Us

Aastha has been working on themes, concepts scripting & choreographing wedding sangeet, wedding anniversaries and parties in an innovative style, which blends the story line with Dance & Drama for more than 5 years.

Trained in contemporary, salsa, Bollywood, punjabi, semi- classical dancer since. Dancing since a kid, has given me numerous opportunities to explore my talent. Recently awarded as the best choreographer with ISOE Academy. Depending on the client needs, we brainstorm with ideas and conceptualise the Sangeet Dance accordingly. From small to big, done it all.

The idea is to create a night of remembrance where every family member is involved equally to show wonders on stage. We ensure to get the best out of you and put across to the audience in a beautiful way.
